Advantages of UPI Payment

  • Instant real-time payments directly from your bank account.
  • Facilitates seamless peer-to-peer and peer-to-merchant transactions.
  • Secure, with two-factor authentication and UPI PIN.
  • No need for physical cards or remembering card numbers.
  • Works 24/7, including weekends and holidays.
  • Enables easy tracking of all payments in one app.
  • No additional charges for UPI transactions.
  • Accepted across numerous merchants, services, and utilities.
  • Enables payment requests to be initiated by merchants for authorization.
  • Allows easy linking of multiple bank accounts in a single app.
